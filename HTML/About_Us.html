Who Are We?

We are an organisation that prides itself in our work, we work to provide people with essentials to keep them warm and keep them safe. Our work will be designed to keep people happy and warm, it is in our nature to help others so that one day they too can help those in need.



What are our Key Concepts

We pride ourselves with certain key concepts that we strictly follow:

Honesty
Honor
Integrity
Kindness

These concepts are there not as a guide but as a rulebook to follow, we understand that it is important to follow these concepts. These concepts are set in stone and are factors that we tend to follow on a daily basis.
We hope that you will be able to assist us one day so that we may assist others in need.
